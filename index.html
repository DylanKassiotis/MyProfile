    Project Idea's

    Overview


    My project idea is a study aid application named Moleti (Greek word for study). Moleti will help students with memorization by having a flashcard and Pomodoro timer functionality. Secondly, Moleti will also have notetaking that would allow the user to reflect upon their notes in the Cornell notetaking method. And Moleti will have organizational benefits by allowing the user to make their study calendar to evolve around their life schedule. As a result of these functionalities students should be able to memorise the information for longer and be able to reflect upon there notes at ease after there studies.

    Motivation

    I believe many of the features of Moleti will have a beneficial impact on decision making, productivity and memorisation of its users. For instance, (Danziger, Levav and Avnaim-Pesso, 2011) reveal in a study that during daily proceedings, the likelihood of a favourable conviction from a judge will gradually fall around 65%. However, after the judge has had recess the likelihood of a favourable and fair conviction will rise by around 65%. Secondly, (Ariga and Lleras, 2011) did a study that uncovers that the decrementation of focus during long task’s such as studying is avoidable by implementing brief breaks where you focus your attention on a secondary task.

    Description

    Moleti will revolutionize the way you study, available on desktop and online, it will have the feature Pomodoro Method with this feature you will be able to use the popular time management technique developed by Francesco Cirillo. To use this feature, you can click the task section to write down your objectives to complete during the Pomodoro. Meanwhile, set the estimated time to complete that task (note one Pomodoro equals twenty-five minutes). Afterwards, select the task that you would like to complete then start the timer. Once started, work for twenty-five minutes then have a five-minute break when the audio notification system starts. If you do not want to do the pre-set timer you can also customize the time to suit your preference. The second feature Moleti has is the Cornell note-taking system, with this feature you can create a new document and then write in the Cornell style, edit it or change its layout, delete it, download as a pdf, or print and share it. Additionally, you can click the flashiphy button to make the bullet points into flashcards. With the third feature of flashcards, you can download pre-existing online or make your own. The Flashcards are also downloadable to print or share. This is a useful tool for revision and memorization. Lastly, the Organizer feature is an organization tool that can be used to make a mind map, a to-do list, or an org-chart, this feature is ideal for teamwork as you can set objectives and see what your team mates are doing.
